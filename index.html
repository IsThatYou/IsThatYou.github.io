About Me

I am a first-year Computer Science and Mathemtics Master Student at UC Irvine, where I work closely with Sameer Singh on Machine Learning Interpretaions and Natural Language Processing Projects.

Previously I worked as a NLP research intern in Tencent and Applied AI lab at Comcast in DC supervised by Ferhan Ture.

My research experience has been prmiarily on adversarial methods and interpretations of NLP models. But I am very interested in all kind of NLP projects, including dialog systems or language models.

I am also part of the ACM@UCI as I enjoy solving programming problems!
